Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ModMaster

Are these mods safe to use?

Yes, all our mods are thoroughly tested for viruses and malware before being uploaded. We prioritize your device's safety and security.

How do I install modded APKs?

To install modded APKs, you need to enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your device settings. Then download the APK file and open it to install.

Why are my downloads slow?

Download speeds may vary depending on your internet connection and our server load. If you're experiencing slow downloads, try again later or use a different internet connection.

Can I request a specific mod?

Absolutely! You can request specific apps or games using our contact form. We'll try our best to create and upload the mod as soon as possible.
